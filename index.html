BAPS SVM

DESCRIPTION

*With the blessings of Param pujya Pramukhswami Maharaj, the fifth spiritual successor of Bhagwan Swaminarayan, H.H. Mahantswami Maharaj (Sadhu Keshavjivandas) has inaugurated a school in 2006 for boys to provide quality education along with virtues in divine ambience in Bakrol, Vallabh Vidyanagar.
*The BAPS Swaminarayan Vidyamandir - Bakrol is well equipped with all modern amenities and has a range of facilities required to create an atmosphere for holistic development of children.
*SVM Bakrol comprises Higher Secondary with science and commerce stream, secondary high school- Gujarati and English medium and Primary school in Gujarati and English medium.
*The school has a modern auditorium along with library, computer lab and science laboratory.
*It is affiliated with Gujarat State Education Board.

ABOUT BAPS SANSTHA

*Bochasanwasi Akshar Purushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha (BAPS; IAST: Bocāsaṇvāsī Akṣar Puruṣottam Svāminārāyaṇa Saṇsthā), is a Hindu denomination within the Swaminarayan Sampradaya.
